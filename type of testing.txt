Unit testing:
1.Unit testing is the execution of a complete class,
routine or small program that has been written by a
single programmer or team of programmers, which
is tested in isolation from the more complete
system.

2.Developer

3.white box testing

Integration testing:
1.Integration testing is the combined execution of two
or more classes, packages, components, or
subsystems that have been created by multiple
programmers or programming teams. 

2.testers and tests integration

3.both white and black box testing
